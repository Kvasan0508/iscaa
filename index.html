ISCCA-2023

International Conference on Intelligent Systems of Computing, Communication and Applied Informatics
(Online Conference)

Our Goal

The goal of ISCCA-2023 is to give academic and professional researchers a forum to discuss cutting-edge developments in the fields of computing, communications, and applied informatics. This gathering will be a fantastic venue for collaborating, showcasing high-end research, and exposing them to renowned speakers from around the world with latest emerging technologies & the most recent innovations in the fields of applied informatics, communication, and intelligent systems of computing and their applications (Advanced in Computing & Communication, Big Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IOT, Informatics Intelligence and Robotics).
